Our Small Business SEO Packages Include -

There are plenty of services that we can help you with when you buy one of our affordable small business SEO packages, including:

Initial Review & Analysis: Our process begins with a comprehensive initial review and analysis to establish a strong foundation for your SEO strategy. We conduct an in-depth site analysis to examine your website's performance and identify areas for improvement. Our keyword analysis helps us discover the most relevant and effective keywords for your business. Additionally, we check for duplicate content, analyze your competition, and perform a local SEO audit. We also investigate any potential Google penalties and identify broken links, ensuring your website is fully optimized and ready for success.

On-Page Optimization: Our SEO team enhances your website's structure and content to boost visibility and performance. This involves canonicalization, optimizing header (H1) tags, improving internal link structures, and refining title and meta tags. We also implement geo-targeting through code and set up GA4/GTM if it hasn’t been configured yet.

Local Search Optimization: We focus on optimizing your Google Business Profile (GBP) by assisting with setup, verification, and optimization. This includes uploading and optimizing images and ensuring that your Name, Address, and Phone number (NAP) are consistent across all platforms. We also monitor your GBP profile for duplicate listings to maintain your business's credibility.

Content Marketing: We produce compelling content that attracts and engages your target audience. This includes article posting, blog posting, guest blogging, and press releases, all aimed at strengthening your online presence and driving meaningful interactions.

Off-Page Optimization: Our off-page strategies are designed to build your website’s authority. We focus on outreach through blogging, engaging in Q&A platforms, creating profiles, and analyzing competitor links to increase your site’s reputation and relevance.

Monthly Reporting: We offer detailed monthly reports that track your progress and performance. These reports cover key metrics such as rankings, traffic stats, SEO activities, and insights from your Google Business Profile, ensuring you stay informed about your website’s growth.

Customer Support: We provide continuous support through our project management tracking tool, ensuring you have full transparency. Our team is available via email, chat, and phone to assist you with any questions or needs you may have along the way.
