Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Pay-Per-Click (PPC) advertising?

Companies using the Pay-Per-Click (PPC) online advertising model get charged each time a user clicks on one of their ads. To drive targeted traffic to their websites, businesses can bid for ad positions on search engines and other platforms.

Is PPC effective in 2025?

Of course! PPC is still an essential part of any digital marketing plan. It is a useful tool for reaching new clients and increasing conversions since it provides immediacy, accurate targeting, and a quantifiable return on investment.

How much does a PPC campaign cost?

A PPC Campaign’s price varies according to the industry, level of competition, and available targeting choices. On average, small and mid-sized enterprises will spend between $300 and $100 million a month, which includes agency fees and ad spend.

How is AI changing PPC in 2025?

PPC is transforming because of AI and machine learning, which automate processes like budget allocation and bid adjustments, enable dynamic ad production, improve audience targeting, and provide tailored ad experiences.
