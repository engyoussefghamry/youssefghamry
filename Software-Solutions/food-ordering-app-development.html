Food Delivery Mobile App features

We all know that mobile app features must be kept in mind while making an app for some business. Mobile Coupons

The app would provide some of the frequent and new users with attractive mobile coupons from time to time to keep the users happy and increase the use of the application. That is how we can make them our regular and permanent customers. In addition, the feature for mobile coupons never fails to fetch new customers.

Social Media Integration

It is one of the most trending features for the application. Everyone is active on social media these days and, it is a great platform to reach an endless audience. The users can upload and share app contents on all kinds of social media that would help in the promotion of the mobile application.

Push Notifications

It is important to remind people about your presence. Hence, this app feature would send notifications and messages to all the users who would have downloaded the application about all the new discounts and places opened and the exciting offers that would increase the engagement of users with your application.

Live Tracking

People have become furious and, they cannot wait to receive their food orders. Live tracking is all that they want now. After placing an order, the users can track their orders. It helps the users by letting them know about the confirmation and status of their orders and about the delivery person who is bringing their order.

Order Scheduling

Your food delivery app would help the users and the restaurants to increase their encouragement with one another by scheduling the orders beforehand.

Menus

People love to see varieties and options available to them. Your food delivery app will provide the menus of all the places linked to the mobile app, which would be very beneficial for the users and for the restaurants as well.

Photo Gallery

Your customers would always be interested in checking out the restaurant, cafes, and all before visiting or ordering food from that place. The app we will deliver would have many interesting features and one of them being the photo gallery. The app will have a photo gallery to help the users to have a glance at the food and aura of the different restaurants, cafes, and rest of the food places so they can choose wisely.

Location

The app we would design would use the user’s location and show the most appropriate places likely to visit according to their previous visits. In addition, the customers can check out the location of a particular restaurant and then plan to go there accordingly. Assisting your customers with the location feature could be a great choice.
