All-in-One Payment Gateways

These providers offer comprehensive solutions that handle everything from payment processing to fraud detection and settlement. They often include a wide range of features to support various business models.

Innovate With Us

Stripe

A powerful and developer-friendly platform offering a full stack of payment services. Key features include:

  • Stripe Terminal: Hardware and software for accepting in-person payments.
  • Stripe Identity Verification: A service for verifying the identity of your customers.
  • Stripe ACH: Direct bank debits and credits.
  • Stripe Marketplace / Stripe Connect: Solutions for platforms and marketplaces to manage payments for multiple sellers.
  • Stripe Hold Money: The ability to authorize and hold funds on a card before capturing the payment.

Moneris

A Canadian-based payment processor offering in-person, online, and mobile payment solutions, including POS systems and e-commerce integrations.

PayPal

A globally recognized platform known for its ease of use and wide consumer base. It offers a variety of business solutions, including online checkout, invoicing, and recurring payments.

Braintree

Owned by PayPal, Braintree is a payment gateway focused on a developer-friendly approach, supporting a wide range of payment methods and offering advanced fraud protection.

Airwallex

A global fintech company that provides a full suite of financial solutions, including a payment gateway that supports multiple currencies and local payment methods for businesses operating internationally.

Eway

A global all-in-one secure payments platform for businesses to accept credit card payments.

Authorize.net

A long-standing payment gateway provider in the US, allowing merchants to accept credit cards and electronic checks through their website

Mollie

A European payment service provider that simplifies online payment processing, offering a user-friendly API and various payment methods like credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

TAP (Tap Payments)

A payment gateway with a strong focus on the MENA (Middle East and North Africa) region, providing a wide array of local and international payment options.

CCAvenue

A popular Indian payment gateway that offers a high number of payment options, multi-currency processing, and advanced security features.

Revolut Payment

Offers a suite of payment solutions for businesses, including online and in-person payment acceptance with low transaction rates and next-day settlement.

NMI (Network Merchants Inc.)

A highly customizable payment gateway that acts as a backend for many other processors, known for its flexibility, security, and global reach.

EMS (Electronic Merchant Systems)

A payment processor offering a range of solutions, including in-person POS systems, mobile processing, and online payment gateways.

2Checkout

A global payment processing solution that enables merchants to accept payments from customers worldwide, offering a localized buying experience.

Digital Wallets and Alternative Payment Methods

These are specific payment methods that can be integrated into a payment gateway. They often provide a faster and more secure checkout experience for customers.

Explore More

Apple Pay

A mobile payment and digital wallet service for Apple devices, allowing users to make secure payments in apps, on the web, and in physical stores.

Google Pay

A digital wallet platform and online payment system developed by Google to power in-app, online, and in-person contactless purchases on mobile devices.

Klarna

A “buy now, pay later” (BNPL) provider that allows customers to split their payments into installments, increasing purchasing power for shoppers.

Afterpay

Another popular BNPL service that lets customers pay for their purchases in four interest-free installments.

Payment Processing Technologies and Concepts

This category includes the underlying technologies or functionalities that make payment processing possible.

Partner with Us

ACH (Automated Clearing House)

A secure electronic network for financial transactions in the US. It’s often used for direct deposits and bill payments and is known for its lower transaction fees compared to credit cards.

Stripe os and andrios

This is likely a reference to the operating systems or SDKs (Software Development Kits) that Stripe provides for integrating its payment services into mobile applications, particularly for Android devices.

Stripe ach

A specific service offered by Stripe to process payments through the ACH network.

Stripe Hold Money

The process of authorizing a payment to ensure funds are available and then capturing the payment at a later time. This is a common feature in many payment gateways.
