ABN Registration Australia needed a faster, more reliable platform to streamline ABN and related business registrations. The site faced issues like slow page speed, malfunctioning forms, failed payments, outdated code, and cache errors. We optimized performance by removing unnecessary code, improving caching, and updating stylesheets. Forms were rebuilt with robust validation, ensuring error-free data collection. Secure Payment Gateways (Stripe, PayPal), Cards like (Debit, Credit, Visa, MasterCard, Amex, and JCB) were integrated for international transactions. The result: a modern, efficient, and trustworthy platform delivering a smooth user experience for entrepreneurs and businesses.
