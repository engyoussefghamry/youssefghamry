Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the purpose of NLP?

NLP is used to analyze language in speech and text. NLP tools help people with daily tasks like text classification, emailing, and understanding foreign languages.

In AI, what does NLP stand for?

The broad topic of artificial intelligence (AI) research focuses on how machines can comprehend our environment. NLP, which focuses on comprehending human language, is only a subfield of artificial intelligence.

What is NLP's primary objective?

Achieving a level of language processing that is comparable to that of humans is the primary objective of natural language processing. It should be capable of classifying, translating, and making sense of the text.

What are NLP solutions?

NLP solutions enable computers to understand and process human language, offering capabilities like text classification, extraction, summarization, predictive text, smart assistants, and improved search engine results.
